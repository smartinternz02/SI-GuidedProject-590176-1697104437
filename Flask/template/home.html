Thyroid Disease Prediction

Your thyroid creates and produces hormones play a role in many different systems throughout your body. When your thyroid makes either too much or too little of these important hormones, it’s called a thyroid disease.There are several different types of disease, including hyperthyroidism, hypothyroidism, thyroiditis Hashimoto’s thyroiditis.
